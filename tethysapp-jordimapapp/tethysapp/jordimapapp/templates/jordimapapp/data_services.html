Data Services

The Data Services used in this app include shapefile data that was originally downloaded at gis.utah.gov, and then modified in ArcMap 10.5 in order to encompass all of Utah County. The data includes municipal data for each municipality within Utah County, as well as DEM raster datasets for the area covering Utah County. With these datasets, we plan on creating a pipe trenching tool. Our shapefiles have been published on the class Geoserver found hosted at the link below.
